## Q5(a) — AS 9 revenue recognition (5) — 1 each

1. Trade discount / volume rebate — **not revenue**, reduction of cost, deducted
2. Sale to distributors — recognise if significant risks passed; buyer in substance an agent → consignment
3. Concurrent repurchase agreement — financing, **not revenue**
4. Insurance agency commission — on effective commencement/renewal date of policies
5. X mart — sale complete on 11.3.2019 (title + billing accepted), recognise FY 2018-19 despite delivery 12.4.2019

## Q5(b) — AS 5 change in accounting policy? (5) — 1 each

(a) 2%→3% doubtful debts — change in **estimate**, not policy
(b) formal gratuity scheme replacing ex-gratia — substantially different transaction, **not** a change in policy
(c) furniture life 5→3 years — change in **estimate**
(d) new pension scheme — new events, **not** a change in policy
(e) change in inventory cost formula — **IS** a change in accounting policy
