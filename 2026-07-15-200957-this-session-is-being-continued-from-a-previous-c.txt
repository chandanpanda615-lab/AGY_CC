● Two steps, and only the first one is yours:

  1. Drop the files in place.

  - Same series as before (another Master Test 01 or 03 copy): make a folder

  it. That's it.
  - New series (a paper we haven't seen): make a new folder at the root, put the
  question paper PDF, the model answer PDF, and the student folders inside it.
  Filenames don't need tidying — I find the paper by Question in the name and
  the key by Model_Answer.

  CC_Law/

      Priya/                ← new folder, named after the student
        scan_8801.pdf       ← her scan, any filename

  has no _evaluated.pdf yet and tell you who I picked.

  From there the whole thing runs end to end: I read every page, check nothing
  is missing from the scan before awarding anything, mark against the series'
  scheme.md (built once, so copy #1 and copy #40 face the same standard — for a
  new series I build it first from the model answer), stamp the copy, generate
  the REMARKS and FEEDBACK pages, render the result and read it back, and write
  the marks.csv row.

  One copy per command, deliberately — you stay calibrated on every copy instead
