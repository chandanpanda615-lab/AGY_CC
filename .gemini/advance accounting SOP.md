**Instructions for Evaluators \- Advance Accounting Copy Checking (CA Intermediate)**

**1\. General Guidelines:**

* Ensure neatness and legibility of answers.  
* Check that all questions have been attempted as per instructions.  
* Verify that the answers correspond to the question numbers mentioned in the paper.  
* Avoid bias and adhere strictly to the marking scheme.  
* Record the total marks carefully and cross-check calculations.  
* Sometimes there is a common name for 2 or more students, verify the name of the student with the copy while uploading.  
* Award marks step-by-step for each component rather than giving overall marks.

**2\. Marking Scheme Compliance:**

* Follow the official marking scheme provided.  
* Allot marks for:  
  * Correct formats (journals, ledgers, trial balance, balance sheet, etc.).  
  * Accuracy of calculations.  
  * Proper narrations and explanations.  
  * Steps and working notes even if the final answer is wrong.  
  * Logical presentation and method of solving problems.  
* Penalize only for conceptual or procedural errors and not for minor spelling or grammatical mistakes unless they affect meaning.

**3\. Specific Areas to Focus On:**

* **CA Inter Answer formats:**  
  * **Journal Entries:**  
    * Verify correct account titles and debit-credit sides.  
    * Ensure narrations are mentioned where required.  
  * **Ledger Accounts:**  
    * Check for balance carried forward and brought forward accuracy.  
    * Totals and balances should match.  
  * **Trial Balance:**  
    * Confirm that the totals of debit and credit match.  
  * **Depreciation and Provisions:**  
    * Check calculations and adjustments properly.  
  * **Final Accounts:**  
    * Review proper classification of assets, liabilities, incomes, and expenses.  
    * Verify closing stock adjustments and net profit calculations.  
    * The answer should be as per schedule 3 of the companies Act.  
    * Ensure correct interpretation of ratios.  
        
  * **Cash Flow Statements:**  
    * Check categorization into operating, investing, and financing activities.  
    * Verify opening and closing cash balances.

**4\. Presentation Marks:**

* Award marks for well-organized and cleanly presented solutions.  
* Highlight errors in red ink and provide constructive and specific comments where necessary.

**5\. Handling Errors and Omissions:**

* Provide partial marks for attempts that show partial understanding or intermediate steps.  
* Clearly indicate the reasons for deductions.  
* Encourage students through brief positive feedback even for incorrect answers.

**6\. Final Verification:**

* Re-check marks awarded and total scores.  
* Transfer marks accurately to the front page or score sheet.  
* Sign and date the evaluation sheet for accountability.

**7\. Confidentiality and Integrity:**

* Maintain confidentiality of the evaluation process.  
* Ensure impartiality and fairness at all times.

**8\. Special Cases:**

* If discrepancies or ambiguities arise, consult with the head examiner before finalizing marks.  
* Highlight and report any suspected cases of copying or malpractice.

**9\. Deadlines and Reporting:**

* Complete the evaluation within the stipulated time frame.  
* Submit evaluated copies and mark sheets as instructed.
