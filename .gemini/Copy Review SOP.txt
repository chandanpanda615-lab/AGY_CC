Copy Review SOP

Use Portal: Review Copy Using WorkFlow Portal.
Verification: Match the checked copy with the actual copy submitted by the student.
Consistency and Fairness: Ensure marks are awarded fairly and consistently across all questions. Ensure that every question is thoroughly evaluated, adhering strictly to the model answers provided (Along with steps marking).
Clarity of Comments: Comments should be clear, specific, and constructive, offering valuable feedback, particularly when deducting marks.
Relevance of Deductions: Check if mark deductions are justifiable and supported with clear explanations.
Accuracy of Marking: Verify that the marks allocated match the evaluation, and check calculations and totals. 
Comprehensiveness of Feedback: Ensure all aspects of the answer are addressed, including content, structure, and clarity.
Encouragement: Ensure feedback includes positive reinforcement along with areas for improvement for students.If required, students can connect with SMEs.
